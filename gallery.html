Curtains the musical

I was one of two ALD's for Curtains the Musical. The challenges we faced and overcame was traveling with the show. This is especially challenging because we moved from an in home LED system and were limited on our options. This refers to the colors we could use, the space we were filling and the amount of special effects we were cable of. We worked on re-cueing the show in order for it to fit the new theatre restrictions.
